Checkersland - play checkers with computer or other people over the Internet.

This site provides different kinds of free checkers programs and services for them. The most powerful version is Checkersland PC that allows you to play more than 20 kinds of checkers. It can be run on the following OS: Windows, Linux, MacOS and Solaris and allows to play with other people over the Internet.
